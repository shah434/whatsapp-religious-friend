# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Jain families across all generations — grandparents who've observed fasts for decades, parents managing household dietary rules, and younger members learning the traditions. They reach for WhatsApp instinctively, not a dedicated app. They need answers that are precise and personalised to their level of observance.

## Product Purpose

Samta is a WhatsApp-based AI assistant for the Jain community. It answers questions about fasting rules, tithi, sunrise/sunset timing, and Jain-friendly restaurants. It exists because dietary rules in Jainism are precise, vary by tradition and strictness, and change daily — and the existing options (Google, family WhatsApp groups, scattered websites) are slow, inconsistent, or impersonal. Success looks like a family member trusting Samta the way they'd trust a knowledgeable elder.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, spiritual. Unhurried authority. Trusted like a family elder, not branded like an app.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages (dark hero, floating cards, "supercharge your workflow" copy)
- Temple / religious websites (heavy gold, saffron overload, ornate patterns)
- Food delivery apps (Zomato/Swiggy energy, food photos everywhere)
- Corporate healthcare (blue-grey sterile, clinical)

## Design Principles

1. **Earn trust through precision.** The community relies on exact answers. Copy, layout, and information hierarchy should communicate care and accuracy, not enthusiasm.
2. **Unhurried over urgent.** No countdown timers, no "sign up now" pressure. The pacing should feel like a thoughtful conversation.
3. **Warm without ornamentation.** Warmth comes from voice and typography, not decorative motifs, gold accents, or saffron palettes.
4. **Show what it does.** Don't describe the product in abstractions. Be specific: tithi, fasting rules, Paryushana, restaurant strictness levels.
5. **Family first, product second.** This is a family project serving a community. The brand should feel personal and rooted, not polished and distant.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Legible at all sizes for older users. No motion without reduced-motion fallback.
