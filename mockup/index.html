What we believe

"Samta — समता — equanimity. The belief that every living being deserves the same gentle regard."

Why we are doing it

A small act of keeping their light alive.

We grew up in the warmth of two people who believed, with every fiber of their being, that every living thing deserves gentleness.

Our grandmother followed Jainism — a quiet, deep commitment to not harming a single soul, not even the smallest ant. Our grandfather lived with kindness — always helping others and lifting people up.

Samta is our love letter to them. A small act of keeping their light alive.

We know living by these values isn't always easy. That's why we're here. Whether you're vegan, vegetarian, or simply trying to be more intentional about how you live your life — we hope this makes that journey a little easier.

Love,
Anish and Parth
